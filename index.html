About

Contract game created by DeFi Hunters DAO with passive yields in BNB directly to your wallet.
All players are placed randomly in 16 levels with unlimited cycles.
Rewards are distributed as follows:
- 74% of level value for each of your level cycle
- 13%-8%-5% of the level value from partners down to 3 in-depth when they activate levels.
